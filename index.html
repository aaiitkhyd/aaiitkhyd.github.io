About SMC

Startup Master Class (SMC) is an initiative by the Alumni Association of IIT Kanpur. SMC is a unique format startup event “for Entrepreneurs, from Entrepreneurs”.

Startup Master Class came into existence 4 years ago with a mandate of fostering and nurturing budding entrepreneurs. It brings together startups, mentors and investors to build interactions and strengthen the network within the entrepreneurial community.

Startup Master Class Platform is a fully volunteer driven, not for profit with a network of passionate individuals and institutions (alumni of PAN IIT network) combining their skills and resources in support of the activities to promote entrepreneurship.

SMC Hyderabad

Zero to One: Take a Leap

Entrepreneurship is not for the weak hearted. You need guts to take a leap at it.

The 10th edition of SMC at Hyderabad, themed "Zero to One: Take A LEap" will center around entrepreneurs, investors and the ecosystem who had the courage to take a leap and seeing it through the various phases of growth. It will witness participation from Alumni, Mentors, Investors, Incubators, Corporates and Startups.

Main Stage

Hear it from the experienced lot. Their stories will help entrepreneur in all stages to assess growth and scale to IPO stage. Learn from the innovators who have left a mark. The problems that they faced and the way they solved them. Also, understand the intellection of the investors who decide to invest money and resources in a raw idea.

  • Early, Growth and Scale Stages
  • Q&A session for the inquisitive minds
  • Fireside Chat

Each startup/participant will get networking opportunities with PAN-IIT alumni, VCs, incubators and accelerators. We will also provide a platform to startups for pitching in front of a 7-8 VC panel at once.

  • Listen to exciting speakers sharing their thoughts in panel discussions and talking about their journey
    • Entrepreneur stories - Learn from the ones who have been there and done that.
    • Investor journeys - What makes them back some entrepreneurs and not others?
  • Attend focused sessions on latest innovations and business models around Hardware, IoT, AI/ML and Blockchain
  • Get the best of 1:1 sessions to connect with experienced mentors and investors
  • Participate in SMC Select to pitch to business experts and investors live on stage.
  • Network with Investors including IIT alumni from all over the world and access the large PAN IIT Global Network

Hackathon

Emerging technologies like blockchain, AI and IOT push the envelope not only in the technical realm but also create hitherto unfathomable opportunities for the next generation of businesses to leverage.

Alumni Association, IIT Kanpur with Trillion coworking and the Hacking School is proud to host a 30 hour hackathon on 10 Feb, 2018. If you think you have what it takes to build the next disruptive tech startup, do register for the Deep Tech Hackathon.

We will have an experienced team of mentors with both technical and business expertise to help make your idea happen. This will also be an amazing opportunity to network with like minded individuals who dare to dream, much like yourselves. Rest Assured, all your mortal needs ( food, caffeine, entertainment ) will be taken care of so that you can concentrate on building the future. Look forward to seeing you there!!!

One-on-One

Face to face discussion with several Angels, VCs and CEOs. Facing dilemma whether or not to startup? Facing initial challenges in your startup? Pitch your idea and get mentored from the masters.

  • Mentorship
  • Informal Pitch
  • Several Angel Investors, VCs and CEOs

One-on-ones are one of the most popular attractions at Startup Master Class that allow entrepreneurs in various stages of starting up a new venture to have a 10-15 minute face to face discussion with investors, industry veterans and mentors.

SMC Select

  • Get evaluated by a panel of investors
  • Only top few startups will get to pitch

  • SMC Startup Select is open for all.
  • The first 50 start-ups who register will get an edge in getting short-listed.
  • Top 12 startups will get to pitch
