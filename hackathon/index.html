Frequently asked Questions

Who can participate?

Developers, Designers, Growth Hustlers…

Pretty much anyone who is excited about the intersection of bleeding edge technologies and the next generation of businesses which will power the world. Both students and professionals are welcome.

We’re looking for people who “do” and are passionate enough to work on crazy world-changing ideas. This is a especially a great opportunity for tech-based startups to gain valuable mentoring and feedback.

If you think you have it, this the chance you have been waiting for.

How does the application process work?

Just click on the register button and fill the form that follows. Responses of this form will be the sole criteria for selection for the hackathon participation. We will contact you on the email ids provided to confirm your participation in the event, once you’re selected.

We believe in giving everyone a fair chance to prove their worth. Tell us your motivations to apply, what excites you, accomplishments you are proud of and whatever else you think can strengthen your chances of acceptance.

When is the application deadline?

The deadline is February 7th at 11:59pm IST. We evaluate applications on a rolling basis and acceptances will be sent at regular intervals. The sooner you apply, the better chances you have of acceptance. The final round of acceptance will be sent on February 8th.

How much does it cost to attend?

Nil. Nada. Nothing. Admission to the Deep Tech Hackathon is completely free, thanks to our partners! We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of swag, meals, drinks, snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from hacking.

Is this a team-based hackathon? Can individuals apply?

While we are open to participation of individuals, the max team size is 2 and we believe our participants can benefit from collaboration. Team combinations like developer + designer, frontend + backend, growth hustler + full stack can hit the sweet spot. If you get selected as an individual and are in need of a teammate you can partner up with others like you on the day of the event.

Once I am accepted, what do I need to bring?

A valid government issued photo ID, laptop, phone, chargers. While this goes without saying, please don’t bring weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If you’re not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!

What about hardware?

If you have specific hardware in mind, bring it with you. Upon selection, we will try to extend support for generic hardware, if needed.

How do I apply to be a volunteer or mentor?

If you're looking to be a volunteer or mentor (i.e. someone with technical skills who'd be willing to help out participants during the weekend), you should reach out to us at meraj@thehackingschool.com.

What will I gain from attending the Deep Tech Hackathon ?

Only the chance of a lifetime! While the major attraction is the chance to pitch your dream startup to a panel of angel investors and business honchos at ISB, the takeaway is much larger. Get validation for your ideas, mentoring, the opportunity to socialize with the startup and hacker communities, not to mention all the free food and schwag ! We assure you that the experience is something you will remember for a long time.

I have questions not covered in the FAQs.
