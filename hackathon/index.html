About Hackathon

The next generation of Indians have taken to technology like fish to water and the Startup Master Class team wants to take them to the next level. We live in exciting times with the spirit of revolution in the air.

Emerging technologies like blockchain, AI and IOT push the envelope not only in the technical realm but also create hitherto unfathomable opportunities for the next generation of businesses to leverage.

Alumni Association, IIT Kanpur with Trillion coworking and the Hacking School is proud to host a 30 hour hackathon on 10 Feb, 2018. If you think you have what it takes to build the next disruptive tech startup, do register for the Deep Tech Hackathon.

We will have an experienced team of mentors with both technical and business expertise to help make your idea happen. This will also be an amazing opportunity to network with like minded individuals who dare to dream, much like yourselves. Rest Assured, all your mortal needs ( food, caffeine, entertainment ) will be taken care of so that you can concentrate on building the future. Look forward to seeing you there!!!

Hackathon FAQs

Who can participate ?

Developers, Designers, Growth Hustlers…

Pretty much anyone who is excited about the intersection of bleeding edge technologies and the next generation of businesses which will power the world. Both students and professionals are welcome.

We’re looking for people who “do” and are passionate enough to work on crazy world-changing ideas. This is a especially a great opportunity for tech-based startups to gain valuable mentoring and feedback.

If you think you have it, this the chance you have been waiting for.

How do I participate ?

Just click on the register button and fill the form that follows. Responses of this form will be the sole criteria for selection for the hackathon participation. We will contact you on the email ids provided to confirm your participation in the event, once you’re selected.

We believe in giving everyone a fair chance to prove their worth. Tell us your motivations to apply, what excites you, accomplishments you are proud of and whatever else you think can strengthen your chances of acceptance.

When is the application deadline?

The deadline is February 7th at 11:59 pm IST.
We evaluate applications on a rolling basis and acceptances will be sent at regular intervals. The sooner you apply, the better chances you have of acceptance. The final round of acceptance will be sent on February 8th.

How much does it cost to attend?

Nil. Nada. Nothing.
Admission to the Deep Tech Hackathon is completely free, thanks to our partners!

We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of swag, meals, drinks, snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from hacking.

Is this a team-based hackathon? Can individuals apply?

While we are open to participation of individuals, the max team size is 5 and we believe our participants can benefit from collaboration. Team combinations including developer + designer, frontend + backend, growth hustler + full stack can hit the sweet spot. If you get selected as an individual and are in need of a teammate, you can partner up with others like you on the day of the event.

Once I am accepted, what do I need to bring?

A valid government issued photo ID, laptop, phone and chargers. While this goes without saying, please don’t bring weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If you’re not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!

What about hardware?

If you have specific hardware in mind, bring it with you. Upon selection, we will try to extend support for generic hardware, if needed.

How do I apply to be a volunteer or mentor?

If you're looking to be a volunteer or mentor (i.e. someone with technical skills who'd be willing to help out participants during the weekend), you should reach out to us at meraj@thehackingschool.com.

What will I gain from attending the Deep Tech Hackathon ?

Only the chance of a lifetime!
The chance to pitch your dream startup to a panel of angel investors and business honchos at ISB, the overall takeaway is much larger.
Get validation for your ideas, mentoring and the opportunity to socialize with the startup and hacker communities, not to mention all the free food and schwag ! We assure you that the experience is something you will remember for a long time.

I have questions not covered in the FAQs.

Feel free to reach out to us at meraj@thehackingschool.com or prashanth@lda.ngo.
